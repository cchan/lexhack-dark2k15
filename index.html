LexHack 2014

Congratulations to winners of the first ever LexHack!

  1. First Place (Leap Motion Controllers) - "3D Etch-a-Sketch" by Lyle Cheatham, Andrew Panasyuk, Anthony Wiryaman, Noel Qiao
  2. Second Place (Quadcopters) - "Snail Mail" by James Curry, William Davenport, Greg Derecho
  3. Third Place (Chromecasts) - "ContraBox" by Naomi Wharton, May Tomic, Sylvie van der Weide, Vyshnavi Chunduru
  4. Beginner's Award (KTByte and Treehouse Lessons) - "Maze Game" by Piper Bau, Anna Sanders, Laura Appleby, Irene Terpstra

Thanks to all the participants for coming to our first ever LexHack and building some great projects. You can check out the winning hacks and even more hacks here.

Special thanks to our fantastic sponsors, mentors, and volunteers, and we'll see you next year!
